about us

At Resilience, we are driven by a singular mission: to empower individuals and communities with the tools they need to proactively manage and respond to disasters, specifically focusing on flood detection. Our user-friendly web app provides real-time alerts, resources, and support to keep you and your loved ones safe during challenging times. We believe that by harnessing the power of technology, we can enhance disaster resilience and make a meaningful difference in people's lives.
